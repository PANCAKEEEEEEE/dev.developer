Our Story

In 2022, three passionate software engineers, Alice, Bob, and Charlie, sat together in a small garage in San Francisco. They shared a common dream: to make software development accessible to everyone, regardless of their technical background.

After countless sleepless nights, fueled by coffee and determination, they created the first prototype of dev.pl. Their innovative platform allowed users to build software applications with ease, using a simple drag-and-drop interface.

The team faced numerous challenges, from technical glitches to securing funding. But their persistence paid off when they launched dev.pl in early 2023. The platform was an instant hit, attracting users from around the globe.

Today, dev.pl is recognized as the world's easiest software building company, empowering individuals and businesses to bring their ideas to life with minimal effort. The journey from a small garage to a global powerhouse is a testament to the team's unwavering belief in their mission.
